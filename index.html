Welcome to Palm Living Interiors

Palm Living Interiors was created in 2020 in Dubai by our two British co-founders Nikki Martin and Karen Dougall. We recognized the need for European minimalistic style furniture, that was high quality yet affordable. With a passion and love for aesthetic lifestyle, interior design and home styling, Palm Living Interiors was born.

Palm Living Interiors is the brainchild of Nikki Martin and Karen Dougall, two British entrepreneurs with a keen eye for design and a dedication to creating beautiful living spaces. Our journey began in the vibrant city of Dubai in 2020, where we identified a gap in the market for European minimalist style furniture that was both high-quality and affordable.

Our vision was to blend the sleek, clean lines of European design with the luxurious yet practical needs of modern homes. We believe that everyone should have access to beautifully designed furniture that doesn't break the bank. This vision led us to establish Palm Living Interiors, where we strive to offer pieces that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also functional and durable.

At Palm Living Interiors, we are passionate about the art of interior design. We understand that your home is a reflection of your personal style, and we are here to help you express that through our carefully curated collections. Whether you are looking to revamp your living room, bedroom, or any other space, we have the perfect pieces to suit your taste.

Our commitment to quality is unwavering. Each piece of furniture in our collection is crafted with attention to detail and the finest materials. We work closely with skilled artisans and manufacturers who share our dedication to excellence, ensuring that every item meets our high standards.

In addition to offering a wide range of furniture, we also provide personalized interior design services. Our team of experienced designers is here to assist you in creating a cohesive and stylish look for your home. From selecting the right furniture to choosing the perfect accessories, we are here to guide you every step of the way.

We believe that good design should be accessible to everyone, which is why we are committed to keeping our prices affordable. At Palm Living Interiors, you don't have to compromise on quality or style to stay within your budget. Our goal is to make high-end design available to all, without the high-end price tag.
